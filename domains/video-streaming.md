# Video Streaming Platforms

## Domain Overview

Video streaming platforms deliver multimedia content through network-based streaming services.

Experience in this domain includes development and validation of media streaming applications and understanding system behavior related to video playback and network delivery.

## Engineering & Quality Focus

- video playback behavior
- streaming session stability
- network latency and buffering behavior
- media delivery reliability

## Typical Systems

- video streaming applications
- media playback services
- network-based media delivery systems

## Keywords

video streaming, media playback, buffering behavior, streaming protocols, real-time media delivery
