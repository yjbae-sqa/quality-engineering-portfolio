# Quality Metrics & Coverage Dashboard

## Purpose

Define measurable indicators to evaluate product quality and release readiness.

Quality metrics help teams understand validation coverage and make informed release decisions.

## Approach

Collect testing results and validation data to visualize overall product quality through a coverage dashboard.

## Metrics Focus

- test coverage of critical workflows
- regression validation status
- automated test execution results
- defect trends and validation gaps

## Validation Flow

1. identify critical service workflows
2. collect test execution results
3. calculate validation coverage
4. visualize metrics through dashboards
5. review quality indicators before release

## Example Metrics

- validation coverage ratio
- automated test pass rate
- regression test completion
- unresolved defect count

## Outcome

Improved visibility of product quality and faster decision-making during release cycles.
